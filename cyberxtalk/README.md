# CyberXTalk

**CyberXTalk** is positioned as a **pre-CERT cyber incident intelligence layer**.

It is not intended to replace national CERTs, SIEM platforms, SOAR systems, law-enforcement portals, banking fraud desks, or institutional incident-response teams.

Instead, CyberXTalk focuses on the gap before formal response:

> Ordinary victims, SMEs, and frontline users often report cyber incidents in informal, noisy, multilingual narratives, while formal response institutions require structured, categorized, prioritized intelligence.

CyberXTalk explores how to transform human-generated cybercrime reports into structured incident intelligence for classification, triage, evidence capture, escalation, and response support.

Avoid weak positioning such as:

> We used machine learning for cyber incident reporting.

Stronger positioning:

> We designed a context-aware, multilingual, low-resource cyber incident intelligence framework that transforms informal human cybercrime narratives into structured, actionable intelligence for pre-CERT triage and escalation.
