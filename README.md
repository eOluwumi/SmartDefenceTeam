# 🛡️ SMART CYBER DEFENCE TEAM

## _Cybersecurity Research & Innovation Hub_

Smart Cyber Defence Team is a focused cybersecurity research and innovation initiative dedicated to exploring intelligent, practical, and responsible approaches to modern cyber defence.

The hub brings together academic research, applied artificial intelligence, cybersecurity engineering, and systems thinking to support stronger digital resilience across institutions, enterprises, and critical sectors.

---

## 🎯 Research Themes

- **Malware Intelligence and Detection**  
  AI-assisted research on malware analysis, classification, and detection workflows for improving cyber defence readiness.

- **Insider Threat and Log Intelligence**  
  Exploration of intelligent techniques for analysing system, network, and user activity logs to identify suspicious behaviour and operational risk signals.

- **Cyber Incident Reporting and Response**  
  Design of structured approaches for reporting, classifying, escalating, and managing cyber incidents across diverse organizational contexts.

- **Cybersecurity Awareness and Behaviour Modelling**  
  Research on human-centred cybersecurity, cyber hygiene, awareness improvement, and behaviour-informed risk reduction.

- **Critical Information Infrastructure Protection**  
  Research and systems thinking focused on securing essential digital services across critical sectors.

- **Intrusion Detection Systems**  
  Study and prototyping of intelligent host, network, and hybrid intrusion detection approaches for emerging cyber threats.

---

## 🧭 Mission

To advance cybersecurity research and system design that is:

- Research-grounded
- Context-aware
- Ethically developed
- Implementation-conscious
- Useful for real-world cyber defence operations
